# Security Policy

At Woven by Toyota, we take the security of our systems and data very
seriously. We appreciate the efforts of security researchers in
helping us maintain a secure environment.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in any of our
systems or products, we encourage you to report it to us promptly. We
kindly request that you follow our official [Vulnerability Disclosure
Policy](https://woven.toyota/en/vulnerability-disclosure-policy/) for
all submissions.

This policy outlines the scope of our program, how to report
vulnerabilities, what information to include in your report, and what
to expect from us during the disclosure process.

Thank you for helping us keep Woven by Toyota secure.
